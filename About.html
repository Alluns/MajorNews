This website was originaly created and managed by a student named Allan Bäckman.

This site originaly started as a school project but later the creator, Allan, fell in love with the project and made it his passion project. Some days Allan would do nothing but polish and expand his news site, he could work for hours on end.

Sadly Allan passed away later that same year after a hit and run at the local coop. After this incident we, the moderator team, decided that the site would focus on happy news in honor of Allan.

Major News is a non proffit, anti-fake news news site designed with mobile first in mind. we always deliever the happiest and most relevant news this site of the galaxy. If you would like to support our efforts you can find a donation link bellow.
